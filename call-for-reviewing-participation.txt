We invite people from the field to participate in the public reviewing
process of this year's edition of the HSDIP workshop.

All submitted papers are available online at:
https://openreview.net/group?id=icaps-conference.org/ICAPS/2019/Workshop/HSDIP

Everyone with an OpenReview account can post comments to a paper. 
Reviews by the program committee will presumably be available from April 5.

We look forward to interesting discussions.


### Dates

Official reviews due: 5 April 2019
Notification: 12 April 2019
Workshop: 11/12 July 2019
